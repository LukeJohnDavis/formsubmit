Animal shelters in Durban save dogs from mistreatment and starvation.


You know those stray/abandoned dogs you see aimlessly wandering the streets every once in a while? All of us at Rescue Marshals, want to save them and provide them with a home.
We try to save as many stray cats and dogs as we can. We are devoted to finding homes for each and every one of them.


Donate to Rescue Marshals so we can continue taking care of and sheltering your favourite breeds of dogs!
If you are interested in following through with a donation, please see the “donations” link at the top of this page.
